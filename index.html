AU-NZ Longsword – Probabilistic Strength & Seeding Model

How to read this table

  • Ranks AU/NZ longsword fencers by estimated competitive strength, rather than published rating alone.
  • Applies a Bayesian-style adjustment that pulls higher-uncertainty ratings toward the population mean, while preserving more of a fencer’s own rating when their Glicko-style deviation is low.
  • Interprets the source dataset’s confidence value as a Glicko-style uncertainty measure, where lower values mean higher trust and higher values mean less certainty.
  • Groups athletes into quantile-based tiers, with + / – markers indicating whether a fencer sits near the top or bottom of their tier.
  • Uses a heatmap to make relative strength across the field easier to compare at a glance.
  • Shows uncertainty (σ) as a derived display value, where lower values indicate a more reliable estimate.
  • Includes Win % as the expected result against the dataset’s average-strength fencer, providing a simple baseline for competitive advantage.
